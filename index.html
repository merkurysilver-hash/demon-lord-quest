Firebase Setup

This character sheet uses Firebase for real-time sync. One-time setup — takes about 5 minutes.

Step 1 — Create a Firebase Project

  1. Go to console.firebase.google.com
  2. Click Add project, name it anything (e.g. "raven-quest")
  3. Disable Google Analytics (not needed), click Create project

Step 2 — Create a Realtime Database

  1. In your project, go to Build → Realtime Database
  2. Click Create Database, choose any location
  3. Select Start in test mode, click Enable

Step 3 — Get Your Config

  1. Go to Project Settings (gear icon)
  2. Scroll to Your apps, click the </> Web icon

What she knows about herself:

She fears her destiny. The title of ‹Demon Lord› carries centuries of blood.

She fears the Hero. Not from cowardice, but from clarity.

She is petty. Small grievances stick.

She is contrary for the sake of being contrary.

She is not as worldly as she pretends.

She is still deciding what it means to be the ‹Demon Lord›.

Core want: To be free. Not a piece on someone else's board.

The Oath▼
"For my truth it is this. I am the Demon Lord. Saddled with a destiny I did not want. And I worry that I will never deserve to be called 'good' because of it."

I exhale slowly. And then take another breath.

"I vow it here and now: though I was born to shatter this world, I shall love it nonetheless. I will stand defiant before gods and men alike, wielding my strength not to tear asunder, but to mend. This world, with all its flaws and beauty, will know my name — not as the harbinger of destruction, but as its savior. I shall forge this world a better future, and I shall suffer neither man nor god to change my course."
Echo — The Eclipse Princess▼

Echo is the second soul inhabiting Raven's body, born from ‹Shattered Self›. She is Raven as she would have been without the ‹Demon Lord› class.

A divergent self — same root, different branch. Colder. More royal. More distant. More logical.

They are not enemies. They are not perfectly harmonious. They are two women who know each other more intimately than anyone ever could.

Echo can speak into Raven's mind at any time.
