Firebase Setup

Real-time sync between devices. One-time setup — about 5 minutes.

Step 1 — Create a Firebase Project

  1. Go to console.firebase.google.com
  2. Click Add project, name it anything
  3. Disable Google Analytics, click Create project

Step 2 — Create a Realtime Database

  1. Build → Realtime Database → Create Database
  2. Choose any location, select Start in test mode

Personality▼

What she knows about herself:

She fears her destiny. The title of ‹Demon Lord› carries centuries of blood. She knows what is coming.

She fears the Hero. Not from cowardice, but from clarity. The fate of Demon Lord and Hero is specific and personal.

She is petty. Small grievances stick. She can hold a grudge over something trivial while being capable of profound compassion.

She is contrary for the sake of being contrary. Tell her what to do and her first instinct is the opposite.

She is not as worldly as she pretends. A sheltered princess who ran away from home, doing her best. The gap between the performance and the reality is a source of both comedy and genuine vulnerability.

She is still deciding what it means to be the ‹Demon Lord›. She knows what it has meant. She does not yet know what it will mean when it is her.

What she shows the world:

A strong front. Fear and weakness are not freely shown. She is not too proud for vulnerability with those she trusts — with Ascenia, Kaela, and Fel, the armor has begun to come down. She is spirited — alive in a way that feels genuine. She is driven more by sentiment than logic or the pursuit of power.

Her core want: Raven wants to be free. Not philosophically — specifically. Not a piece on someone else's board. Not her father's, not Gray's, not the System's, not the world's inherited story of what a ‹Demon Lord› must be.

The Oath▼
"For my truth it is this. I am the Demon Lord. Saddled with a destiny I did not want. And I worry that I will never deserve to be called 'good' because of it."

I exhale slowly. And then take another breath.

"I vow it here and now: though I was born to shatter this world, I shall love it nonetheless. I will stand defiant before gods and men alike, wielding my strength not to tear asunder, but to mend. This world, with all its flaws and beauty, will know my name — not as the harbinger of destruction, but as its savior. I shall forge this world a better future, and I shall suffer neither man nor god to change my course."
Echo — The Eclipse Princess▼

Echo is the second soul inhabiting Raven's body, born from ‹Shattered Self›. She is Raven as she would have been had she never received the ‹Demon Lord› class — had she grown into the ‹Eclipse Princess› that might otherwise have been her birthright.

She is not a separate person. She is a divergent self — the same root, a different branch.

Where Raven is warm, alive, and sentiment-driven, Echo is colder — more reserved, less given to open emotion. More royal — the bearing of a princess who grew up performing royalty rather than running from it. More distant — where Raven reaches toward people, Echo maintains careful space. More logical — less driven by feeling, more by calculation and considered principle.

They are not enemies. They are not perfectly harmonious. They are two women who know each other more intimately than anyone ever could — because they are each other.

Echo can speak into Raven's mind at any time regardless of manifestation.

The Night of the Class Assignment
On Raven's fifteenth birthday, the System assigned her ‹Demon Lord› — the first in centuries. Knowing her father would use it, she fled into the Labyrinth of Murder and swore her oath: not to destroy the world, but to protect all that made it beautiful.

The Labyrinth of Thirsting Joy
Raven confronted the manifestation of her inner desire and overcame it through compassion. She gained ‹Shattered Self› and Echo, forced the Incarnation of Pride to submit, ascended to ‹Demon Lord of Pride (2)›, and received ‹Epochbreaker (1)›.

Scholomance — The Arcanum
Enrolled with ‹Demon Lord› concealed. Befriended Ascenia, Kaela, and Fel. Spent several months building genuine friendships and deepening her magical studies.
