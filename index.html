The Oath▼
"For my truth it is this. I am the Demon Lord. Saddled with a destiny I did not want. And I worry that I will never deserve to be called 'good' because of it."

I exhale slowly. And then take another breath.

"I vow it here and now: though I was born to shatter this world, I shall love it nonetheless. I will stand defiant before gods and men alike, wielding my strength not to tear asunder, but to mend. This world, with all its flaws and beauty, will know my name — not as the harbinger of destruction, but as its savior.
